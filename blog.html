About us


Purobani group of companies was the dream of a Social Entrepreneur born out of his passion to do business with a purpose and build relationships in the journey. The current version of the organization continues with the similar passion and purpose and intertwines the core value to give back to society in the Real estate development sector and also facilicate wellness programs to stay healthy and happy. Enabling the management to focus on things that matter most i.e. “Feeling for Community, Making housing affordable, excel in customer service, innovative ways of staying happy through implementation of ancient practices” . We work at the focussing on your need to stay cosy, stay safe, stay happy – Whatever are life principles are, we want to enable you to get there through Excellence, Care, Competitive Edge and Operational Efficiency.”

Our journey would focus on enabling each of our units with a measure of Annual Happiness Quotient and we look forward to our country measuring Gross Domestic Happiness alongwith Gross Domestic Product
